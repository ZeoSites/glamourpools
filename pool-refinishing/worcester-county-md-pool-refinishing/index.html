Common Pool Issues and Solutions

When dealing with pool usage and management, common pool issues are prone to arise, they are difficulties that occur in situations where a shared or common resource is used by multiple individuals or groups.

Surface Cracks and Damage

Cracks and damage to the pool surface are common, influencing the pool's aesthetics and safety. When this happens, thoroughly inspect the pool surface, repair any cracks promptly, and contemplate resurfacing using durable and crack-resistant materials like epoxy or fiberglass coatings.

Fading and Discoloration

The pool's finish can fade or discolor due to exposure to UV rays, chemicals, or aging. Choose high-quality, UV-resistant resurfacing materials and consider additives that deter discoloration. Maintain proper water chemistry to decrease chemical impact on the surface.

Adhesion Problems (Peeling or Delamination.

Improper adhesion of the resurfacing material can lead to peeling or delamination over time. Ensure correct surface preparation, including thorough cleaning and prepping of the existing surface, and follow the manufacturer's guidelines for material application to boost adhesion.

Algae Growth and Staining

Algae growth and staining can occur on the pool surface, making it unattractive and difficult to clean. You can select algae-resistant resurfacing materials and ensure correct chemical balance and regular cleaning to avoid algae growth and staining.

Rough or Uneven Surface

Renewing the surface can sometimes result in an unequal or bumpy texture, influencing the pool's aesthetic and comfort. If this happens, employ expert professionals like Glamour Pool Resurfacing Services, Worcester County MD, who can apply the resurfacing material evenly and smoothly, ensuring a comfortable and attractive pool surface.
